About Us

**ROY TECH LAB** was founded in **February 2025** with the mission to bring **robotics** and **STEM education** to schools in India. Our first successful collaboration was with **Ramakrishna Mission Vivekananda Vidyamandir**, where we introduced hands-on robotics learning for students.

We aim to empower the next generation with **future-ready skills** by setting up labs, training teachers, and running robotics programs tailored to school curriculums.

What We Teach in Robotics

Year 1: Building the Foundation (Classes 5 to 12)

  • Introduction to hardware components
  • Introduction to basic software for Arduino coding and simulation tools (e.g., Tinkercad)
  • Introduction to microcontrollers (Arduino Uno)
  • Introduction to basic sensors

Year 2: Advancing with IoT, 3D Printing, and AI (Classes 5 to 12)

  • Internet of Things (IoT) projects
  • 3D printing
  • AI-based projects
  • Advanced Arduino projects

Note:

If your institution needs it, we can also provide a fun and easy curriculum for **Classes 1 to 4**.

Project Examples

  • LED blinking (using code)
  • Line-following robot
  • Obstacle-avoidance robot
  • PIR motion sensor alarm circuit
  • IR sensor-based alarm circuit
  • And many more (based on students’ interests)
